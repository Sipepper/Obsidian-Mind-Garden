# Submodule
***
###### tags: #Algebra  
***
>[!dsn] Direct strict note
>Let $W$ be a non-empty set of an $R$-module $V$, then $W$ is called the *submodule* of $R$-module $V$ if it's closed under operation of addition of multiplication by a scalar.
