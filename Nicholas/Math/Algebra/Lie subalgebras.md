# Lie subalgebras
***
###### tags: #Algebra 
***
>[!dsn] Direct strict note
>Let $L$ be a Lie algebra. The subspace $A$ of $L$ is said to be a *subalgebra* of algebra $L$, if $[a,b]\in A$ for all $a,b\in A$. In another words, the subspace $A$ is a subalgebra of $L$ if 
>$$[A,A]\subseteq A$$
>Subalgebras often denoted as $A\le L$. If $A$ is a proper subspace $A<L$.
