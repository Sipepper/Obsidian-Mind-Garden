# Leibniz subalgebras
***
###### tags: #Algebra 
***
>[!dsn] Direct strict note
>Let $L$ be a Leibniz algebra, a subspace $A$ of $L$ is said to be a *subalgebra* of algebra $L$, if $[a,b]\in A$ for all $a,b\in A$. In other words subspace $A$ of $L$ is a subalgebra of $L$ if
>$$[A,A]\subseteq A$$
>Denoted as $A\le L$.
