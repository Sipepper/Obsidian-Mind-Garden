# Sylow p-subgroup
***
###### tags: #Group_Theory 
***
>[!dsn]+ Definition
>Let $S$ be a maximal $p$-subgroup of a group $G$, i.e. $S$ is not contained in any other $p$-subgroup. Then such group is called *Sylow $p$-subgroup*.
