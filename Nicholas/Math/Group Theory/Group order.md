# Group order
***
###### tags: #Group_Theory 
***
>[!dsn]+ Definition
>Let $G$ be a group, then the cardinality $|G|$ is said to be the *order* of group $G$.

>[!example]+ 
>- $|\mathbb{Z}|=\infty$ i.e. $\mathbb{Z}$ has an infinite order.
>- $D_{n}$, the dihedral group, has an order of $2n$.
