# Torsion-free group
***
###### tags: #Group_Theory   
***
>[!dsn]+ Definition
>Group $G$ is called the *torsion-free* if every non unit element has an infinite order.

>[!example]+ 
>As an example, additive group $\mathbb{Z}$ of integers is a torsion-free group.
