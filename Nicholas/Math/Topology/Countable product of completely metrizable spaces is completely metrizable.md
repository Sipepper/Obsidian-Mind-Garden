# Countable product of completely metrizable spaces is completely metrizable
***
###### tags: #Topology 
***
>[!dsn]+ Proposition
>Let $(X_{i},\tau_{i})$, $i\in\mathbb{N}$ be a countably infinite family of completely metrizable spaces. Then $\prod_{i=1}^{\infty}(X_{i},\tau_{i})$ be a completely metrizable space.^[[[Sidney A. Morris - Topology without tears.pdf#page=233|Sidney A. Morris - "Topology without tears" p.233]]]
