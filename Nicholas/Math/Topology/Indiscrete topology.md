# Indiscrete topology
***
###### tags: #Topology 
***
>[!dsn]+ Direct strict note
>Let $X$ be any non-empty set and $\tau=\{X,\emptyset\}$. Then $\tau$ is called the *indiscrete topology* and $(X,\tau)$ is said to be an *indiscrete space*.^[Sidney A. Morris - "Topology without tears" p.27]
